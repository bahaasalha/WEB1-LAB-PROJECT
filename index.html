About The Islamic University of Gaza, known as the IUG

Indonectetus facilis leo nibh

IUG is an independent Palestinian university established in 1978 in Gaza City. View the IUG site here.

It was the first higher education institution to be established in the Gaza Strip., The university has eleven faculties capable of awarding BA, BSc, MA, MSc, MD, PhD, and higher diplomas.

For more information faculties IUG Faculties.


Boards and Organizing Bodies: The university has a number of boards that support, organize and direct its academic mission. The most important are:
The Board of Trustees.
The University Council.
