Distinguished colleges and universities in the Gaza Strip

Israa University-Gaza (IUSG) is an independent, non-for profit and private Palestinian University, which established in 2014 by a group of pioneers, academics, and resaerchers who shared a vision.

About 350 faculty members and more than 1000 academic appointments in affiliated teaching in universities, schools, clinics.read more »

Founded in 1998, UCAS is a Palestinian academic institution of higher education in the Gaza Strip that offers bachelor's and diploma degrees in more than 80 distinguished technically and vocationally oriented programs.

Accredited by the Palestinian Ministry of Education and Higher Education as a University College, and working with top-level institutions, our teaching and research meet the highest local and international standards.

Focusing on applied and vocational education, we aim at increasing and promoting knowledge, and serving the Palestinian community in particular. read more »
